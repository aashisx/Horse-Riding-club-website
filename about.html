About Everest Horse Riding

Our History

A Legacy of Passion

Everest Horse Riding Club was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate equestrians who shared a dream of bringing world-class horse riding experiences to the breathtaking landscapes of Nepal.

What began as a small stable with just four horses has grown into one of the region's premier equestrian facilities, now home to over 20 well-trained horses and a team of experienced guides and instructors.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of introducing thousands of visitors to the joy of horse riding, from complete beginners taking their first steps to experienced riders seeking adventure on challenging mountain trails.

Our Mission

Creating Unforgettable Experiences

Our mission is to provide safe, enjoyable, and authentic horse riding experiences while maintaining the highest standards of horse care and environmental responsibility. We believe that the bond between horse and rider creates moments of pure magic, and we're dedicated to making those moments accessible to everyone.

Horse Welfare

Quality Experience

Every ride is carefully planned to provide an exceptional experience, with attention to detail and personalized service.

Our Commitment

Horse Care & Safety

At Everest Horse Riding Club, the welfare of our horses is our top priority. Each horse in our stable receives individual attention and care, including daily grooming, proper nutrition, and regular veterinary check-ups.

We carefully match riders with horses based on experience level and weight, ensuring both comfort and safety for horse and rider. Our horses enjoy generous rest periods between rides and ample time in the pasture.

Safety is paramount in all our operations. Every rider receives a comprehensive safety briefing, proper protective equipment, and is accompanied by trained guides who are certified in first aid and emergency response.
