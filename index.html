Welcome to

Everest Horse Riding Club

Nestled in the heart of Nepal's stunning landscape, Everest Horse Riding Club offers an unparalleled equestrian experience. Whether you're a seasoned rider or a complete beginner, our expert guides and well-trained horses will take you on an unforgettable journey through the majestic Himalayan trails.

Since our founding in 2010, we've been dedicated to providing safe, enjoyable, and authentic horse riding experiences while maintaining the highest standards of horse care and environmental responsibility.
